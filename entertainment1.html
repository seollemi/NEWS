LOOK: BINI, G22 showcase Filipino talent in China's 'Show It All'

Josiah Antonio, ABS-CBN News
P-pop girl groups BINI and G22 showcased Filipino talent during their appearance in the China-based show "Show It All."

"Show It All" is a reality show in China that features Chinese performance trainees getting ready, learning and training to become an idol group.

During the episode, BINI performed "Karera" and "I Feel Good" with member Aiah Arceta noting how they will treasure this moment as a group.

"I think this experience will be something that we will always carry and treasure with us because being able to come here in China, it's a first for us," she said on the show.

Meanwhile, G22 brought their A-game with "Babalik" and "Boomerang" describing the experience as overwhelming yet fulfilling at this early stage of their careers.

“We went there to help and inspire their Chinese girl group, but we ended up learning a lot from them as well. It was new, it was fun, it was also nerve wracking at times. We definitely came back with a whole set of skills, awareness and patience,” said G22 member Jaz.
