Rayver Cruz shares BTS vid, reveals funny bit behind of Julie Anne San Jose’s ‘Piliin Mo Ang Pilipinas’ entry

Rayver Cruz shares BTS vid, reveals funny bit behind of Julie Anne San Jose’s ‘Piliin Mo Ang Pilipinas’ entry

By GMA Integrated News
Rayver Cruz was present when Julie Anne San Jose filmed her viral “Piliin Mo Ang Pilipinas” entry!

On his account, the supportive boyfriend shared a compilation of the behind-the-scenes clips he took at the studio, revealing Julie Anne was wearing slippers underneath her spruced up FIlipiniana look.

“Syempre may bts ako,” Rayver said laughing.

Julie Anne commented, “Love ung tsinelas” with laughing emojis.

As of this writing, Julie Anne’s video has already reached almost 9 million views and over 2 million likes.

She joined the TikTok trend dressed as Maria Clara, the role she played in the historical portal fantasy series "Maria Clara at Ibarra."

The Kapuso series first hit TV screens in 2022 on GMA Telebabad and, due to its popularity, later graced streaming platform Netflix, where it can still be viewed today.

It also starred Dennis Trillo, David Licauco, and Barbie Forteza.
